# **App Name**: Elite Looks Booking

## Core Features:

- Service Listing: Display a comprehensive list of all available beauty services including Hair treatment, pedicure, manicure, acrylic, facial treatment wig making, wig installation, press on nail, tidy and polish, touch up, washing of hair, pony tail, finger waves, straightening of hair, Waxing, full body piercing, eye lush extension and hair coloring with clear descriptions.
- Stylist Profiles: Showcase individual stylist profiles (Gifty, Fuseina, Pearl, Oprah) with their expertise and the services they specialize in, aligning them with relevant service offerings.
- Booking Calendar: Integrate a real-time booking calendar that displays availability for each stylist and service, enabling users to select preferred dates and times.
- Online Booking: Facilitate online booking, enabling customers to conveniently schedule by picking date and time. Send confirmation emails after booking.
- AI Style Suggestion: Based on a client provided image of their desired look, use a tool to recommend stylists and hair colors best suited to the desired image, as well as suggest similar styles. Limit options to styles the salon can produce.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Saturated purple (#9C27B0) to evoke sophistication and beauty, linking to historical references of royalty and elegance.
- Background color: Very light purple (#F3E5F5), desaturated to 20% to maintain focus on content while providing a soft, elegant backdrop.
- Accent color: Pink (#E91E63), brighter and more saturated to highlight CTAs and key information.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', serif font known for elegant and intellectual feel
- Use elegant and minimalistic icons representing various beauty services.
- A clean, grid-based layout to present information in an organized and easily navigable manner.
- Subtle transition animations and hover effects to add a touch of sophistication.
